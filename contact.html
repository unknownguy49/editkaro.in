Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What types of video editing services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of video editing services including short-form videos for social media, long-form content for YouTube, gaming videos, sports edits, eCommerce product videos, documentary-style editing, color grading, anime edits, and commercial advertisements.

How long does it take to complete a video editing project?

The timeline depends on the complexity and length of the project. Short-form videos typically take 1-3 days, while more complex projects like documentaries or heavily edited content may take 5-7 days or more. We'll provide you with a specific timeline after discussing your project requirements.

How do I share my footage with you?

You can share your footage with us through cloud storage services like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer. For larger projects, we can also arrange alternative file transfer methods. We'll provide specific instructions after you contact us.

What are your payment terms?

We typically require a 50% advance payment to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion before final files are delivered. For long-term collaborations or larger projects, we can discuss custom payment schedules.
